3 CONTINENTS TOURISM SPOT



"ASIA"


"TOURIST SPOTS IN PHILIPPINES"




SAIRGAO

A surfer's paradise, Siargao offers great waves and a laid-back atmosphere. It's also known for the stunning Sugba Lagoon.




LUNETA

Rizal Park, also known as Luneta Park or simply Luneta, is a historical urban park located along Roxas Boulevard
on Manila. It is adjacent to the old walled city of Intramuros and
is of the largest urban parks in Asia. It has been a favourite leisure spot and is frequented on
Sundays and national holidays.




BORACAY

Famous for its powdery white sand beaches and vibrant nightlife, it's a top destination for water sports and relaxation.




"TOURIST SPOTS IN JAPAN"




TOKYO

The bustling capital city is known for its neon-lit streets, historic temples, and shopping districts like Shibuya and Shinjuku.




KYOTO

Famous for its well-preserved traditional architecture, historic temples,
and beautiful gardens. Don't miss Kinkaku-ji (the Golden Pavilion) and Fushimi Inari Shrine.




MOUNT FUGI

Japan's iconic mountain is a must-visit. You can hike during the climbing season or enjoy views from the surrounding lakes and hot springs.




"TOURIST SPOTS IN KOREA"




JEJU ISLAND

Known as the "Island of the Gods," Jeju is famous for its stunning natural landscapes,
including beaches, waterfalls, and the volcanic Hallasan Mountain.




SEOUL

The capital city is a vibrant metropolis with a rich history. You can explore historic palaces like Gyeongbokgung,
visit markets like Namdaemun, and enjoy the bustling nightlife in areas like Itaewon.




MIRYANG

Famous for its stunning cherry blossoms in spring, you can enjoy the picturesque landscapes
and visit the Miryang Arirang Station, known for its beautiful architecture.

KRAKOW

Krakow: Known for its well-preserved medieval old town, the Wawel Castle, and the beautiful Main Market Square.




WROCLAW

Wroclaw: Explore the picturesque Market Square, historic architecture, and visit the famous Wroclaw Gnomes.




"TOURIST SPOTS IN UNITED KINGDOM"




LONDON

London: The capital city is a must-visit with iconic landmarks like the British Museum,
Buckingham Palace, the Tower of London, and the West End theaters. Don't forget to take a ride on the London Eye.




STONEHENGE

Stonehenge: This ancient stone circle is one of the world's most famous prehistoric monuments and a UNESCO World Heritage site.




CORNWALL

Cornwall: Enjoy the rugged coastline, picturesque villages, and beautiful beaches in this southwestern region of England.




"TOURIST SPOTS IN BELGIUM"




BRUSSELS

Brussels: The capital city offers a mix of historic and modern attractions. Visit the Grand Place, Atomium, and enjoy Belgian chocolates and waffles.




BRUGES

Known as the "Venice of the North," Bruges is famous for its well-preserved medieval architecture, canals, and charming streets.




GHENT

Ghent: Explore the historic city center with its medieval buildings, Gravensteen Castle, and the picturesque Graslei and Korenlei canals.




"AMERICA"




"TOURIST SPOTS IN CANADA"




TORONTO,ONTARIO

Toronto, Ontario: Canada's largest city, home to the CN Tower, Royal Ontario Museum, and diverse neighborhoods like Kensington Market.




JASPER NATIONAL PARK, ALBERTA

jasper National Park, Alberta: Another stunning Rocky Mountain destination with glaciers, lakes, and abundant wildlife.




OTTAWA, ONTARIO

Ottawa, Ontario: Canada's capital city is home to historic landmarks, including Parliament Hill and the National Gallery of Canada.




"TOURIST SPOTS IN BRAZIL"




RIO DE JANEIRO

Famous for its iconic Christ the Redeemer statue, Copacabana and Ipanema beaches, and the vibrant Carnival celebrations.




SAO PAULO

Sao Paulo: Brazil's largest city offers a bustling urban experience, cultural attractions, and a dynamic food scene.




SAIRGAO

One of the most awe-inspiring waterfalls in the world, straddling the border between Brazil and Argentina.




"TOURIST SPOTS IN MEXICO"




CANCUN

Cancun: Known for its beautiful beaches, crystal-clear waters, and a lively nightlife scene.




SAN MIGUEL DE ALLENDE

San Miguel de Allende: A charming colonial town known for its picturesque streets, art galleries, and cultural festivals.




PUERTO VALLARTA

A popular coastal destination with beautiful beaches and a lively downtown area.
